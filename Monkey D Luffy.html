Monkey D.Luffy

Monkey D. Luffy, also known as "Straw Hat Luffy" or simply "Straw Hat" ("Straw Hat"), is the protagonist of the One Piece anime and manga series. The founder captain of the Straw Hat Pirates, and one of the top four fighters.
His dream is to become the Pirate King and find the legendary One Piece treasure left by the late Pirate King Gol D. Roger. He believes that becoming the Pirate King will make him the freest man in the world. He ate the Devil Fruit rubber rubber fruit and gained the ability to stretch his body like rubber. Following his invasion of Totland and his actions against the Four Emperors Big Mom, he is now considered the Fifth Emperor by the world's press.
