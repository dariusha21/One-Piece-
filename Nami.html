Nami

Nami is a pirate and navigator of the Straw Hat Pirates. She officially became the third member of the crew and the second to join the crew after Luffy's defeat of Arlong. During the Baratie Arc, she temporarily leaves the team, but returns at the end of the Arlong Park Arc after her past and true intentions are revealed. Her current bounty is 66,000,000.

Appereance

Nami is a young, slender girl of average height with red hair and light brown eyes. Many people consider her very attractive or even beautiful. On her left shoulder is a blue tattoo of a stylized pinwheel and tangerine, which was given to her in place of her mark indicating her affiliation with the Arlong Pirates.
